Mission

To teach is to touch a life forever. Our mission here is to provide meticulous and focused training to the aspirants to succeed in the exams conducted by the Teacher Recruitment Board (TRB). Also to create a platform where the students have access to all resources, both online and offline, that enable themselves to prepare for TRB and other exams effectively, efficiently, and enthusiastically, thereby ensuring them with excellent coaching and the finest study materials.

Courses Offered

PG Assistant Training:

Aspiring to be a PG Assistant, and don't know where to start? You are at the right place. The Post Graduate Assistant Training program offers completely personalized training and coaching process for you to come out with flying colors in the PG assistant Exam conducted by the TRB. Walk-in to our center to enroll.

PG Assistant Training - Online:

If you don't want to or won't be able to come to our center due to geological reasons, we also offer you an online PG Assistant Training Program, where you get access to a complete guide for preparation, periodic tests, and Full Mock Tests. 24/7 assistance provided through calls. Once you are on board, you also get updates on official announcements, schedules, etc.
