Overview

Education is the future, but we - Educators are the key to the future. In 2012, Bharathi Study Centre was initiated to enlighten and enhance pupils who aim at becoming the educators of the forthcoming generations. Since then, the center has and is proving to be one of the best institutions in TamilNadu for mentoring and guiding thousands of teaching aspirants.

With our experienced instructors and the expertise gained over these years, the center has evolved and come out successful, by providing help and resources for 100+ deserving candidates to realize their dream of becoming a bureaucrat and assisted thousands of others to realize their true potential. We ensure that the efforts of our diligent students do not turn into a wild goose chase by providing them complete assistance from start to the end. Our contribution to the society is small, but the effect of all the skillful teachers we helped put in the society is tremendous. Come, join us in our journey of building the future. All it takes is some determination and smart work.

Vision

Our vision is to serve the society and thereby the nation, by producing eminent and responsible teaching community. It also includes growing in size and expanding our franchise to become a renowned institution, so that we can give hope and opportunities to aspirants from various socio-economic backgrounds to realize their dream of becoming a part of the teaching community.

Mission

To teach is to touch a life forever. Our mission here is to provide meticulous and focused training to the aspirants to succeed in the exams conducted by the Teacher Recruitment Board (TRB). Also to create a platform where the students have access to all resources, both online and offline, that enable themselves to prepare for TRB and other exams effectively, efficiently, and enthusiastically, thereby ensuring them with excellent coaching and the finest study materials.

Courses Offered

PG Assistant Training:

Aspiring to be a PG Assistant, and don't know where to start? You are at the right place. The Post Graduate Assistant Training program offers completely personalized training and coaching process for you to come out with flying colors in the PG assistant Exam conducted by the TRB. Walk-in to our center to enroll.

PG Assistant Training - Online:

If you don't want to or won't be able to come to our center due to geological reasons, we also offer you an online PG Assistant Training Program, where you get access to a complete guide for preparation, periodic tests, and Full Mock Tests. 24/7 assistance provided through calls. Once you are on board, you also get updates on official announcements, schedules, etc.

Polytechnic Lecturer Training:

This training program is for those who are aspiring to be lecturers at Government Polytechnic Colleges. At our center, we deliver intense coaching and assistance for pupils to clear the Polytechnic Lecturer exam conducted by the TRB. The training comes with a structured study plan, interactive classes, and doubt clearance by expert instructors.

Online Test Scheme:

Online Test Scheme enables you to test your knowledge, see for yourselves, how much you have prepared and which part you are lagging at. You get to answer about 200 questions in each unit, and at least 4 complete mock tests in all subjects including General Knowledge and Psychology. The detailed report after each test helps you to keep track of your progress and proceed accordingly.

TET Coaching:

TET online coaching is a smart preparation approach, introduced to help you in fulfilling your dream of clearing teaching exams. Here, we offer you completely FREE materials. Use your time effectively and give a boost to your preparation by solving the practice questions offered in the material.

GATE & UGC CSIR Assistance:

This is one of our initiatives to provide a helping hand for students aiming to get through the GATE and UGC CSIR exam. This program offers you numerous question banks with solutions, discussions of previous year questions and answers, and a lot more.
